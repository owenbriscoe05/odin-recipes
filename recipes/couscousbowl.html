Couscous Bowl

Description

Couscous bowls are a delectable yet simple meal containing healthy choices and an excellent source of protein. They require some preparation and practice, but once perfected, you won't want to cook anything else!

Couscous bowls consist of (you guessed it) couscous, grilled chicken, hummus, and a cucumber/tomato salad. It can also be served with tahini sauce, if desired.

It's important to set up your ingredients before beginning to cook. This, as well as multitasking, will save you a lot of time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Pearl couscous
  • Cucumber
  • Roma tomato
  • Shallot
  • Lemon
  • Dill
  • Hummus (any kind)
  • Seasonings of your choice for the chicken

Steps

  1. Organize ingredients in front of you -- separate salad ingredients from rest
  2. Place a large pan on burner and coat with light layer of butter
  3. Cut chicken breasts in half and season to your desire, minimum salt and pepper
  4. Place chicken in pan to begin cooking
  5. Place couscous in medium-sized pot and add oil + sliced shallot. Place on burner.
  6. Begin preparing salad: Dice tomatoes, soak shallots, slice cucumber
  7. In a medium-sized bowl, combine tomato, shallot, cucumber. Zest lemon and add to bowl. Wash dill, grind, and place in bowl. Add lemon juice for flavor.
  8. Once couscous is toasted, add water, cover pot, and bring to boil
  9. Continue to check chicken -- 160ยบ is recommended
  10. After couscous is brought to boil, let cook 7-8 minutes or until water is all gone
  11. Combine chicken, couscous, salad, and hummus in bowl
  12. Enjoy!
