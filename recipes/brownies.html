Fudgy Brownies

Description

Brownies are an all-time classic. In my humble opinion, the absolute best brownies are fudgy and have maximized chocolatiness. This recipe will be for gooey, chocolatey brownies that will melt on your tongue.

Make sure you have some of your favorite ice creams -- or even just a nice french vanilla -- ready to go to pair with this delectable desert.

Ingredients

  • Granulated sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs + water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients (sugar, flour, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, salt) into medium bowl
  2. Combine wet ingredients (eggs, oil, water) in large bowl
  3. Sprinkle dry ingredients over wet and fold until combined
  4. Pour batter into baking pan lined with parchment paper
  5. Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until toothpick comes out nearly clean
  6. Enjoy!
